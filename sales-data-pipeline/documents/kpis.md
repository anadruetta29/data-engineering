## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Daily Sales: The count of unique sale_id entries per day (filtered for successful payments).
- Revenue by Country: The sum of amount grouped by the country field (from either the Sales or User table).
- % Failed Payments: The ratio of payments with a "failed" status compared to the total number of 
- payment attempts.
- Average Ticket (AOV): The total revenue divided by the total number of successful sales.
- Most Used Payment Methods: A ranking of payment_method based on the frequency of appearance in the 
- Payments source.
